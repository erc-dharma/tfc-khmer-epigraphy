I.

II.

III.

rājye bhikṣuvariṣṭhau stas sodarau sthira - ⏑ -

V.

śubhakīrttir iti jñāto niyuktaś śubha - ⏑ -

VI.
(6) [sva]kulakramasantatyā bhūpateś śāsanena ca
tasmint saṁnyasyate sarvvaṁ gurubhiḥ puṇya - ⏑ -
