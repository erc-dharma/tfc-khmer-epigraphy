K. 509/vie śaka (Tham Prasat)

Pas d'estampage

Seidenfaden 1922, p. 58

Texte [GC] :

I.
(1) naptā śrīsārvvabhaumasya sūnuś śrivīravarmmaṇaḥ
(2) śaktyānūnaẖ kaniṣṭho pi bhrātā śribhavavarmmaṇaḥ

II.
(3) śricitrasenanāmā yax pūrvvam āhatalakṣaṇaḥ
(4) sa śrimahendravarmmeti nāma bheje bhiṣekajam

III.
(5) jitveman ṭeśam akhilaṅ giriśasyeha bhūbhr̥ti
(6) liṅgan niveśayām āsa jayacihnam ivātmanaḥ
