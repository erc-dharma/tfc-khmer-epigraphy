Estampage EFEO : n. 817
Text is given after Coedès, BEFEO XXXI, p. 15 and IC I, p. 50-1; with variant readings after Jacques 2014, p. 114-6, and Bourdonneau et al. (nd), in note; we postpone a decision on which reading is better, or whether some fourth reading is to be preferred.
Wherever Coedès reads 'si', Jacques and Bourdonneau et al. have 'sī'.
