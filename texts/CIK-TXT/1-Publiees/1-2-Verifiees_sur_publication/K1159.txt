K. 1159/ 916 śaka (Ban Pho Sai, Thaïlande)

% KC: The inscription is also known under K. 1169. The text is given after NIC IV, p. 111 and estampage EFEO n. 1702, n. 1703 (K. 1169 : cf. estampage EFEO n. 1482). The inscription has two faces, probably one in Old Khmer and another in Sanskrit. S. Pou edited only the Old Khmer text which she is called "Face A". However, CIK team identifies the Old Khmer face as "B" and gives a preliminary reading of the Sanskrit face. The signs * which are used to indicate the missing akṣaras are kept as they are.
