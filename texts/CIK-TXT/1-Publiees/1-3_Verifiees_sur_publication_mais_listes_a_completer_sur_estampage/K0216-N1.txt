K. 216-N1/927 śaka (Phnom Preah Net Preah)

Piédroit Nord de la nef

IC III, p. 37

Estampage EFEO n. 458

Texte :


(1) || qoṁ || 927 śaka pūrṇṇam(ī) vaiśākha śukravāra @ gi nu khloñ· vala qadhyāpaka (th)vāya

(2) śloka paṅgaṁ thpvaṅ· nivedana ta dhūli vraḥ pāda dhūli jeṅ· vraḥ kamrateṅ· qañ· śrī jaya-

(3) vīravarm(m)adeva teṁ qupāya steṅ· (qa)ñ· vraḥ jrai phoṅa @ cuñ· vraḥ qālakṣa-

(4) (ṇa ta khlo)ña vala khloñ· jnvāl· kanmyaṅ· vraḥ kralā lqvaṅ· cuñ· pandval· ta vraḥ 

(5) sabhā phoṅ· pre mratāñ· śri jayendropakalpa sabhāsat(·) mratāñ·
%a. śri: corr. śrī. 
%b. sabhāsat(·): sabhāpati Cœdès. 

(6) śri jayend(r)apaṇḍita bhāgavata paṁre vāp· deṅ· mūla raṅ·vāṅ· nā triṇi mok ś(r)ā-
%a. śri: corr. śrī. 
%b. vāp· deṅ·: vāp· deva Cœdès; vāp· teṅ· Aymonier. 
%CC: raṅ·vāṅ·: Even if a virāma is clearly visible above the consonant ṅ, those two terms must be understand as a single one (raṅvāṅ·).

(7) la qa(ṁ)pāl· qupāya steñ· vraḥ jrai nuva khñu[[ṁ]] sruk· sre vraḥ kuti vraḥ śrī rā-
%CC: kuti: In Old Khmer, kuṭi is frequently written kuti (and this one is also found in line 10, written kutī). On this subject, see the note in Griffiths and Soutif 2008-2009, p. 38, n. 15.

(8) jendrāśrama nu vraḥ kamrateṅ· qañ· śaṅkara pragalbha ta kulo steñ· vraḥ jrai 

(9) qyat· vakra vva(ṁ) qāc ti qāyatva ta qnak· vraḥ vvaṁ qāc ti qāyatva ta qnak ta khloñ· ni nā

(10) vraḥ @ thve kutī noḥ pūjā kamrateṅ· jagat· roha man· steñ· vraḥ jrai 

(11) ta qji dai kalpaṇā @ dah· (qya)ta kulo steñ· vraḥ jrai leya qāyatva ta (qa)-

(12) dhyāpaka ta kuti noḥ @ nau ruva qupāya nu khñuṁ santāna dai phoṅ· dār· hau qisa

(13) qyat(·) vakra qāyatva ta dai thve kuti noḥ roha man· qoy· saṁnvatta || @
