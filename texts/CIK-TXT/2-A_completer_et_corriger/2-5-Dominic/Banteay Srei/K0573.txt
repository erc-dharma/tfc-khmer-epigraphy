K. 573/ 10th century CE (Banteay Srei, Siem Reap)

Estampage EFEO n. 424

Majumdar, R. C. Inscriptions of Kambuja, Calcutta 1953, no 108D, p. 282. The simple daṇḍas added by Majumdar are removed.

I. [metre: anuṣṭubh]
(1) jayati prathamaḥ puṁsāṁ yasya śavdaguṇaṁ padam· 
(2) paramaṁ paramārthajñais sandr̥ṣṭam iva madhyamam· || 

II. [metre: anuṣṭubh]
(3) tasya yajñavarāhasya saṁvandhī dharmma vāndhavaḥ 
(4) ākhyāṁ śrīpr̥thivīndrādyāṁ paṇḍitāntāmavāpa yaḥ || 

III. [metre: anuṣṭubh]
(5) teneha sṭhāpitā viṣṇoḥ prabhaviṣṇor iyan nimā 
(6) bhaktyā bhāgavatāryyeṇa sarvvaśāstrārthavedinā ||
