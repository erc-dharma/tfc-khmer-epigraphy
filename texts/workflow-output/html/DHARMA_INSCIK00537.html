    Stela from Hospital East of Ta Keo Temple (inscription of hosptital of King Jayavarman VII, K. 537), 1108 Śaka

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-09-14Z

    Editor:   Kunthea Chhom.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK00537

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.
