    Commentary

    33-40From here onwards, S. Pou considers the inscription as illegible. She evaluated 6 lines lost 33-39. Claude Jacques evaluated at least 50 lines in total for this doorjamb. Compared to Pou, I evaluate around 13 more lost lines, bringing the total to 53.
