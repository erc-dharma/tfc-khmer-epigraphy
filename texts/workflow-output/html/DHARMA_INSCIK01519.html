    Stela from Ku Khanthanam, Roi Et province (inscription of hosptital of King Jayavarman VII, K. 1519), 1108 Śaka

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-08-15Z

    Editor:   Kunthea Chhom.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK01519

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.

    Restitutions are based on the almost identical inscriptions K. 12, K. 209, K. 368, K. 386, K. 395, K. 402, K. 435, K. 667, K. 1115, K. 1170, K. 1402, K. 1521 and K. 1524.

    A14There is no space between the pādas c and d.

    B7The almost identical inscriptions of K. 1519 has bhaiṣajyasugataṁ vyadhāt· instead of bhaiṣajya-sugatālayam·.

    D18There is no space between the pādas c and d.
