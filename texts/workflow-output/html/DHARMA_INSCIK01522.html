    Stela from Tham Chan, Si Sa Ket province (inscription of hosptital of King Jayavarman VII, K. 1522), 1108 Śaka

    Editor:   Kunthea Chhom.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK01522

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.
