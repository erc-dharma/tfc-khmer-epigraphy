    Stela from Ku Phon Rakhang, Roi Et province (inscription of hosptital of King Jayavarman VII, K. 1520), 1108 Śaka

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-10-09Z

    Editor:   Kunthea Chhom.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK01520

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.
