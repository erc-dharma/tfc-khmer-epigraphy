    Stela from Tonle Sgnuot Temple (inscription of hosptital of King Jayavarman VII, K. 955), 1108 Śaka

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-10-03Z

    Editor:   Kunthea Chhom.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK00955

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.
