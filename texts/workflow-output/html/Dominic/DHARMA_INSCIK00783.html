Inscription for installation of liṅga by Jāhnavī, Yajñavarāha’s sister, at Banteay Srei (K. 574)

Metadata

Identifier:

INSCIK00783

Summary:

In one anuṣṭubh verse, this inscription records the installation of images of Umā and Maheśvara by Yajñāvarāha, in Banteay Srei.

Hands:

Edition

Anuṣṭubh
3tena yajñavarāheṇa
pitror ddharmmavivr̥ddhaye
4umāmaheśvaranime
sthāpite sthitivedinā ||

Apparatus

1ayajñavarāheṇa• Cœdès mistakenly prints this with a dental n (instead of a retroflex).

Translation into French

1Yajñavarāha, qui connaît les usages établis, a érigé les images d’Umā et de Maheśvara pour l’augmentation des mérites de ses parents.
