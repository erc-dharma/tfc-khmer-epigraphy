Inscription for installation of liṅga by Jāhnavī, Yajñavarāha’s sister, at Banteay Srei (K. 574)

Metadata

Identifier:

INSCIK00574

Summary:

In one anuṣṭubh verses, this inscription records the installation of a liṅga by Jāhnavī, sister of Yajñāvarāha, in the southern tower of the temple-complex of Banteay Srei.

Hands:

Two lines engraved on the southern doorjamb of the southern sanctuary-tower of the complex of Banteay Srei.

Edition

Anuṣṭubh
3tasya yajñavarāhasya
jāhnavīti yavīyasī |
4svasā saṁsthāpayām āsa
bhaktyā liṅgam ihaiśvaram ||

Translation into French

1Jāhnavī, sœur cadette de Yajñavarāha, a érigé pieusement ici un liṅga d’Īśvara.
