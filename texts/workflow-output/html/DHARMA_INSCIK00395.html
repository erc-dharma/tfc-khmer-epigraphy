    Commentary

    Restitutions are based on the almost identical inscriptions K. 209, K. 368, K. 386, K. 402, K. 435, K. 667, K. 1115, K. 1170, K. 1402 and K. 1521.

    Face A is almost damaged; only few words in lines 8-10 and 14-21 can be recognised.

    There are four fragments of Face B covering all the 12 stanzas (st. XIII-XXIV) on this face.

    One fragment of Face C is identified. It contains the first 14 lines of the left column or the pādas a and c of the first 7 stanzas.

    There are four fragments of Face D covering 10 stanzas on this face, leaving two stanzas, XLIII and XLIV.
