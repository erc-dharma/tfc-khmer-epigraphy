Translation by Kunthea Chhom

1-73rd day of wanning moon [of the month of] Vaiśākha (April-May) [...] to V.K.A. [...] Mratāñ Bhagavat, Poñ Kumāramati [...] who were at that(those) place(s) when Poñ Kularakṣa [...] Poñ Bhā Puṣpa, Mratāñ Candravīra, Kan-Et, [...] Poñ śrava, Vrau Et, [...] Si Dat, Kanses, Vā Kmau, Vā Ratna, [...]

8-10Then Poñ Kumāramati gave the paddy-field to the god [...] 2 yau of doubled cloth for the lower garment for V.K.A. [...] the cloth for the lower garment of the superior god [...]

Commentary

1The p of pari does not have a curl at the bottom unlike that of puṣpa in line 5.

1The u of nu does not have a hook like that of nu in line 8.
