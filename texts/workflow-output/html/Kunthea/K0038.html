Apparatus

1 ni vraḥ kaṁmratāṅ· Añ· • The ṅ· of the kaṁmratāṅ· is probably forgoten by the scribe and written very small on the top of the syllable tā.
4 trava⟨ṅ⟩· • The virāma is on the ku as the ṅ is forgotten by the scribe.
