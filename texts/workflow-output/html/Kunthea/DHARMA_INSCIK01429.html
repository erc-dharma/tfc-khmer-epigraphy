Apparatus

8 [vat sūryya] • The restitution yāvat sūryyasya candrasya is inspired from K. 341 which contains the expression yāvat sūryyāscandrasya tāvat naraka dukhitaḥ.

Translation by Kunthea Chhom

1-6[...] offered servants, cows [...and] silver. The paddy field Tnal Teṁ Trāc [...] chanel; the paddy field Vrai Hvāṅ, the paddy field [...] which was cultivated [by] Mratāñ Yajamāna [...] Tāṅ Yajamāna, the paddy field Ramayoṅ [... paddy field] Nojña which Vraḥ Añ Yajamāna offered to [...].

7-10All these paddy fields which have been offered to the god [... Those] who take away the treasure of the god will go to hells; they will suffer in the hells as long as the Sun and the Moon exist.
