    Metadata

    Current Version: 

    draft, 2022-06-06Z

    Editors:  

    Kunthea Chhom.

    Identifier:

    INSCIK01555

    Hands:

    The basic punctuation sign in this text is an ornate double daṇḍa written in one continuous movement, resembling an inverted S-shape. The name of the poet being made known in the text itself, it is possibile that the hand is that of the poet.
