    Commentary

    The estampage BN 286 (33) is not available for checking. However, the Planche 13 of Barth’s edition allows us to check the Sanskrit portion of the inscription.

    Regarding the stanzas 1-4, Auguste Barth comments as follows: the stanzas I-IV provide only some terms which are not really related to one another. Śrī Śrīdharā is a proper name or a part of a name which appears in the stanza IV. What can be understood from the next stanza is that besides the invocation, the stanzas I-IV mention two kings.
