Slab of stone from Vat Sre Ping (K. 911), Angkorian period

Metadata

Identifier:

INSCIK00911

Hands:

Edition

1vraḥ pāda śrī
2vālāditya

Apparatus

1 śrī • It can also be read śri.

Translation by Kunthea Chhom

1-2His Majesty Śrī Vālāditya.

Commentary

The name of King Vālāditya appears also in four Sanskrit inscriptions of the 10th century, i.e. K. 263, K. 528, K. 669 and K. 806.

Bibliography

First edited by George Cœdès (1937-1966: volume 5, page 85) without translation; re-edited here by Kunthea Chhom from the estampage EFEO n.1181.
