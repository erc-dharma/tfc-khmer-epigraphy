1-7[...] to Vāsudeva [...] together with Poñ [... : ] Candradāsa, Ku Miṅmauj, Ku Knoc, Ku Sanrāp, [...], Ku Vreṅ, Ku Sramo, Ku Vlac, [...], Ku Svoy, Ku Kaṁcik, Ku Tmoṅ, [...].
