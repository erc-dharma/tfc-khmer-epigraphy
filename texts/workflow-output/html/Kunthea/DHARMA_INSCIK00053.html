II. Anuṣṭubh

t trivikrama-parākramaḥ _
yasya saurājyam adyāpi _

III. Anuṣṭubh
3tasyābhūtāṁ bhiṣaṅmukhyau _
bhrātarāv aśvināv iva _
brahma-dattas sa yo jyeṣṭho _
brahma-siṅhas sa yo ’nujaḥ

IV. Anuṣṭubh
4tayor api mahābhāgyau _
bhāgineyau vabhūvatuḥ _
dharmmadevaḥ prathamajaḥ _
siṅhadevas tv anantaraḥ

V. Anuṣṭubh
5svaśaktyākrānta-rājyasya _
rājñaś śrībhavavarmmaṇaḥ _
śrīgambhīreśvaro yasya _
rājya-kalpataroḥ phalaṁ

VI. Anuṣṭubh
6tasya tau mantriṇāv āstāṁ _
sanmatau kr̥tavedinau _
dharmma-śāstrārtha-śāstra-jñau _
dharmmārthāv iva rūpinau

VII. Anuṣṭubh
7 mahendravarmmaṇo bhūya _-
ś śrīmataḥ pr̥thivī-pateḥ _
tau cāpy amātyatāṁ prāptau _
pratyayau kr̥tya-vastuṣu

VIII. Anuṣṭubh
8siṅhadevo ’nujo rājñā _
dūtatve satkr̥taḥ kr̥tī _
prītaye preṣitaḥ premnā _
campādhipa-narādhipam·

IX. Anuṣṭubh
9dharmmadevasya tu punaḥ _
tanayo bhūd analpa-dhīḥ _
kula-kānana-siṅho ya _-
s siṅhavīra itīritaḥ

X. Anuṣṭubh
10vidvān yo ’dyāpi vidvadbhi _-
r āpīta-kavitā-rasaḥ _
śrīśānavarmmanr̥pate _-
r abhavan mantri-sattamaḥ

XI. Anuṣṭubh
11nikāma-varadan devaṁ _
śrīnikāmeśvaraṁ haraṁ _
hariñ ca siddhi-saṅkalpa _-
svāminaṁ siddhi-dāyinam·

XII. Anuṣṭubh
12yo ’tiṣṭhipad imau devau _
śraddhayā bhūri-dakṣiṇau _
kīrtti-stambhāv ivodagrau _
yau sthitāv ā bhuva sthiteḥ

XIII. Anuṣṭubh
13tasya sūnur asūyādi _-
doṣair aspr̥ṣṭamānasaḥ _
yo bhavad bhava-sanyasta _-
citta-vr̥ttir udāra-dhīḥ

XIV. Anuṣṭubh
14vālye ’pi vinayopeto _
yauvane ’pi jitendriyaḥ _
trivarggārambha-kāle ’pi _
dharmme yas tv adhikādaraḥ

XV. Anuṣṭubh
15yasminn aidaṅyugīne ’pi _
sadācārāvalaṁvini _
kali-pracālito dharmmo _
na skhalaty ekapād api

XVI. Anuṣṭubh
16śrīmato rāja-siṅhasya _
jayino jayavarmmaṇaḥ _
yo vaidyo veditavyānāṁ _
vettāpi nirahaṅkr̥tiḥ

XVII. Anuṣṭubh
17punas satkr̥tya yaṁ rājā _
prādāt sve rāja-mātule _
Alapdha-rāja-śapde ’pi _
lapdha-rājārha-saṁpadi

XVIII. Anuṣṭubh
18paścād āḍhyapurasyāsya _
yo ’ddhyakṣatve kula-kramāt· _
yogyo yam iti satkr̥tya _
svayaṁ rājñā niyojitaḥ

XIX. Anuṣṭubh
19yasminn avati dharmmeṇa _
parābhyudaya-kāriṇi _
Anvartha-saṁjñāṁ saṁprāpta _-
m idam āḍhyapuraṁ puram·

XX. Anuṣṭubh
20Ucitaṁ yaḥ karādāna _-
m ārāmebhyaḥ kuṭumvināṁ _
Anādadat prabhur api _
pūrṇṇāṁ vr̥ttim adād itaḥ

XXI. Anuṣṭubh
21roginām arthinām vāpi _
visraṁbhād ruṣitaṁ vacaḥ _
śr̥ṇvato yasya karuṇā _
dviguṇā samajāyata

XXII. Anuṣṭubh
22yan madīyaṁ śubhan nāma _
janmaprabhr̥ti saṁbhr̥taṁ _
tad astu pitur eveti _
saṅkalpo yasya kīrttitaḥ

XXIII. Anuṣṭubh
23śiva-yajñena yo devā _-
n munīn addhyayanena ca
pitr̥̄ṁś cātarppayat toyai _-
s satputrakaranissr̥taiḥ

XXIV. Anuṣṭubh
24teneha siṅhadattena _
dattadātavyavastunā _
sthāpito vijayasyāyaṁ _
dātā śrīvijayeśvaraḥ

XXV. Anuṣṭubh
25Asmin tena ca yad dattaṁ _
dāsārāmādi kiñcana _
tad eva devasvam iti _
na haren nāpi nāśayet·

XXVI. Śārdūlavikrīḍita
26vaiśākha-prathama-dvi-pañcaka-dine dvārāṣṭa-vāṇair yyute _
jīvaś cāpayuto vr̥ṣe kavi-sutas siṅhārddhagaś candramāḥ
27kaulīre vaṇijo ghaṭe ravi-sutaś śeṣās tu meṣa-sthitā _-
s so ’yaṁ śrīvijayeśvaro vijayate yaḥ kīṭa-lagne sthitaḥ

Apparatus

6 rūpinau • Barth prints the last word thus: rupinau. He adds a note to the effect that we must understand rūpiṇau. The estampage appears clearly to have rūpinau.
8 premnā • As Barth points out, we must understand premṇā.
12 bhuva sthiteḥ • This sandhi-option (bhuva sthiteḥ = bhuvaḥ sthiteḥ) seems to be only rather rarely followed in Cambodia.
13 bhavasanyasta • As Barth remarks, we should understand bhavasaṁnyasta.
17

Alapdha-rāja-śapde ’pi _
lapdha-rājārha-saṁpadi
• Understand: Alabdharājaśabde ’pi labdharājārhasaṁpadi!
25 Asmin tena • The regular sandhi at the beginning would be asmiṁs tena.
27 kaulīre vaṇijo • In pāda c, Barth proposes to correct kaulīre vaṇijo and to understand instead kaulīre avanijo!

Commentary

Handsome 7th-century lettering (589 ṡaka = 668 AD), but, as Barth notes (p. 66), the jihvāmūlīya and upadhmānīya appear no longer to be in use!
