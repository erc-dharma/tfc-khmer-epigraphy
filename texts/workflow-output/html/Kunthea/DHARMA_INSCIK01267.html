Translation into French

The translation is given after with modifications because of new reading.

1-4Ordre du V.K.A. de donner le village de Pu Dok à Mratāñ Kamratāṅ Craṅgos, le village de Pramān.

5-6Aux gens qui gravent et servent de témoins, un kaṭṭī d’or [est donné].

Notes

Commentary

Jenner (DPAK, p. 146) translates the lines 5 and 6 as: ‘Persons who deface this shall pay a fine of land [and] one catty of gold’
