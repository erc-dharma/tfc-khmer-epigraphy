Commentary

In his edition, GC only mentions that the text contains a list ofchdiṅ“river”,thkval“mound”,vrai“forest” with indications of some quantities of grains, honey, bee-wax which were to be offered. He provides three names of rivers, viz.chdiṅ ransi,chdiṅ thlānandchdiṅ tammriy slāp.
