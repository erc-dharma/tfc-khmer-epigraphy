    Stela Surin National Museum (inscription of hosptital of King Jayavarman VII, K. 1524), 1108 Śaka

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-09-27Z

    Editors:   Cha-em Kaeokhlai and Kunthea Chhom.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK01524

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.

    Commentary

    Restitutions are based on the almost identical inscriptions K. 12, K. 209, K. 368, K. 386, K. 395, K. 402, K. 435, K. 667, K. 1115, K. 1170, K. 1402 and K. 1521.

    Regarding the face B, Kaeokhlai’s editions provide a restitution of the stanzas XIII-XVII rather than a reading of the remaining passages.
