    Doorjamb of the structure PK 12 of Preah Khan temple (K. 462-4), 12th century Śaka

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-10-09Z

    Editor:   George Cœdès.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIK00462-4

    Hand Description:

    The akṣaras are of square form which is characteristic of the script belonging to the reign of Jayavarman VII.
