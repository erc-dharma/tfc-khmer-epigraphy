K. 561/603 śaka (Tuol Ang Tnot, Ang Ta Som commune, Tram Kak district, province de Ta Keo)

Estampage EFEO n. 409

IC II, p. 39.

Texte :

I.
(1) jayaty aśeṣalokānān nātha eko maheśvaraḥ 
pratanoti sadāṣṭābhis tanubhir yyo khilañ jagat 

II.
(2) śrīraṇḍāparvvateśena kr̥taṁ śrījayavarmmaṇā 
miśraṁ śrikhaṇḍaliṅgākhyaṁ trivyomartuyute śake 

III.
(3) śrīkhaṇḍaliṅge yad dattaṁ bhavacandrena yajvanā 
putrapautrair aharttavyam anyaiś ca kulavāndhavaiḥ 

IV.
(4) dravyaṁ śrīkhaṇḍaliṅgasya ye haranti narādhamāḥ 
te vicinarakaṁ yāntu pitr̥bhir vvāndhavais saha 

V.
(5) saṁkhyayā vi[ṅśa]ti vrīher eko bhr̥tyaḥ paṭadvayaṁ 
saṁvatsaram ito dattaṁ śrīraṇḍāparvvateśvare 

VI.

nivandhanañca yas tyaktvā sa tāmisram ito gataḥ
